Privacy Policy

Idle Repair Tycoon · Last updated: 25 June 2026

Idle Repair Tycoon does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect names, emails, contacts, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information. There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect data in this version of the app.

Game data

Your game progress is saved locally on your device only. We have no servers and never receive a copy of your save data.

Purchases

Optional in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see or receive your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple handles transactions.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If our data practices ever change, we will update this page and the App Store privacy details accordingly.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email oleg.ptuh@gmail.com.
